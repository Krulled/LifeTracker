How should the "Add to Log" draft flow work?

When the AI responds with food/calorie data, which interaction feels most natural?

A

Inline meal selector — stays in chat

Button appears below the AI bubble. Click it → a meal picker row (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snack) expands right there in the chat. Pick meal, entry is added instantly. No leaving the chat.

🤖

B

Pre-fill the day panel form

Button appears below the AI bubble. Click it → the right-side day panel opens the inline food form, pre-filled with the AI's extracted data. User picks meal, reviews/edits fields, then hits Add.
