Life Tracker — Skincare AI Orchestration / Design Mockup

What's being built: Two interconnected features — (1) a photo-based product inventory scanner that identifies ingredients and product type via AI, and (2) a daily routine orchestrator that uses your workout log + active inventory to have Claude generate an optimized, safety-validated routine each day.
